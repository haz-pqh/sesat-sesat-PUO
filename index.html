Politeknik Ungku Omar, Ipoh

Politeknik Ungku Omar (PUO), established in 1969 in Ipoh, Malaysia, is the country's oldest and most prestigious polytechnic. PUO offers diploma programmes in engineering, business, and IT focusing on practical and industry-relevant skills. PUO provides various services, including career counselling, industrial training, and entrepreneurship support. The polytechnic also offers short courses, and industry collaborations to enhance employability. With modern facilities and strong industry ties, PUO remains a leading institution for technical and vocational education in Malaysia.

Politeknik Ungku Omar (PUO) offers a wide range of diploma programs in various fields to equip students with technical and practical skills for the workforce. Such as :

  • Engineering & Technology
  • Information Technology & Computer Science
  • Business & Management
